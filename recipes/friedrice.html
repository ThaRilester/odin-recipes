Best Fried Rice in the World

A Quick, easy, and healthy snack the family will love!

This fried rice is a simple but satisfying dish, made with fluffy rice tossed in a hot pan until lightly crisp, then mixed with savory seasonings and fresh ingredients. Itâ€™s versatile enough to enjoy on its own as a filling meal or paired alongside other dishes for a complete plate. Every bite has that comforting balance of flavor and texture that makes fried rice a timeless favorite.


What youre going to grab from the fridge

  • 2 cups day-old cooked rice
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 eggs, scrambled
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, corn)
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced

The assembley process

  1. Heat sesame oil in a pan.
  2. Add garlic and cook until fragrant.
  3. Stir in mixed vegetables and cook until tender.
  4. Push veggies to the side and scramble the eggs.
  5. Add the day-old rice and stir everything together.
  6. Pour in soy sauce and mix well.
  7. Top with green onions and serve warm.
