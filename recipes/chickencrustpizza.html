Protein-Filled Chicken Crust Pizza


Revolutionizing dieting with all time favorite snacks

Everyone has cravings while trying to drop weight and eat clean. Driving past multiple famouse pizza chains on the way home from your job can eb enough to make you start negotiating with yourself and potentially cheat on your diet with far to many carbs then you commited to eating. Now you can have the best of both worlds with this delicious pizza made with ground chicken as the crust! Follwo this delicious recipe ecxactly.

What you'll need

  • 1lb of ground chicken
  • 12oz of freshly made tomato sauce
  • 1/2lb of mozzerella cheese(shredded)
  • 1/2lb of freshly cut pepperoni
  • 1 pinch of freshly chopped parsley (optional)

The process

  1. Start by smashing flat your ground chicken into whatever size/shape you want your pizza to be
  2. cook for 10 minutes at 400 degrees(note it will not be fully cooked, but 80%)
  3. Remove from oven and spread an even layer of your pre seasoned tomato sauce
  4. evenly sprinkly your mozzerella cheese across the top
  5. Strategically place pepperonis all overthe surface
  6. return to oven for an additional ten minutes and then remove
  7. (optional) sprinkle fresh chopped parsley before serving hot
